# Google Flow \- Iteration 2

A fitness app virtual world for VR running experience, the athlete's character is represented by a 3rd person avatar dressed in caveman clothes and the environment is prehistoric forest with prehistoric vegetation, and also dinosaurs. The environment should be photorealistic. As a runnign experience the user can also gather points for gamification. The VR cast has a HUD, which displays the following metrics: distance traveled in miles, current pace, calories burned, HP, and points gathered. Extra health aid kits can enhance the HP or other gathered extras can add points. The runner would see some dinasours, we can have a peek of a vulcano when the thick vegetation is a little bit receded.
